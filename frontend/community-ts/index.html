Common Questions

Everything you need to know

DHub Community is completely free for hosts, volunteers, and charities. We believe the cost of feeding communities should be zero. Our platform is funded through DHub's commercial partnerships.
Every pickup includes a mandatory quality assessment by trained volunteers who rate food as Fresh, Acceptable, or Needs Immediate Distribution. Photos are logged for full transparency and accountability.
Cooked meals, packaged foods, bakery items, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products are all accepted. The food must be prepared within the last 24 hours and be in a condition you'd serve to guests.
Our average response time is 22 minutes. Volunteers within 5km are notified immediately. You can set a pickup window of up to 2 hours so there's no rush.
A two-wheeler is ideal for most pickups. However, for smaller quantities (under 5kg), walking or cycling is perfectly fine. You specify your vehicle type when registering.
Charities submit their registration certificate (12A/80G or trust deed) during sign-up. Our team verifies within 48 hours. Verified charities get priority matching with donors in their area.
